# Terminology

## Purpose

This document defines the official terminology used across Builders
Atlas.

Using consistent terminology improves communication between
contributors, designers, engineers, AI systems, and documentation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Concepts

## Atlas

The overall platform.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Workspace

A geographic knowledge space.

Examples:

-   Qeshm
-   Kish
-   Hormuz

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Entity

A real-world object represented inside Builders Atlas.

Examples:

-   Place
-   Business
-   Person
-   Organization
-   Project
-   Opportunity

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Observation

A raw field observation submitted by a contributor.

Observations are not automatically trusted.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Record

A structured representation of an entity.

A record can be created from one or many observations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Contribution

Any action that improves knowledge.

Examples:

-   Add a photo
-   Correct coordinates
-   Update opening hours
-   Verify information

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Mission

A task generated by the system to improve knowledge quality.

Examples:

-   Photograph a business
-   Verify a location
-   Update land prices

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Verification

The process of confirming that information is accurate.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Confidence Score

The system's confidence in the accuracy of a record.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Knowledge Coverage

Percentage of real-world entities represented in the platform.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Knowledge Graph

The connected network of all entities and their relationships.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Steward

An experienced community member responsible for reviewing and improving
knowledge within a specific domain.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## AI Assistant

The built-in intelligence layer that helps contributors classify,
complete, validate and connect knowledge.

AI never has final authority.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Naming Rules

Always prefer:

Entity

instead of

Row

Use:

Contribution

instead of

Submission

Use:

Observation

instead of

Report

Use:

Knowledge

instead of

Content

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Guiding Principle

Builders Atlas models the real world.

The database is only a representation of reality.

Reality always comes first.
