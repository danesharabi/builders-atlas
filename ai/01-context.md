# 01 - Context

## Purpose

This document provides the context required for AI coding agents to
understand why Builders Atlas exists before implementing any code.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Project Family

Builders Lab ↓ Builders Capture ↓ Builders Atlas ↓ Builders Intelligence

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Builders Lab

Builders Lab is a private community focused on creating meaningful
collaborations through continuous knowledge sharing and experimentation.

It is not an event business.

It is not a social network.

Its purpose is to generate trusted relationships and opportunities.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Builders Capture

Builders Capture is the first product.

Its purpose is to collect structured, verifiable field observations.

The primary output is trusted data.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Builders Atlas

Builders Atlas transforms verified observations into a structured local
knowledge graph.

Atlas is not responsible for collecting data.

It consumes verified knowledge produced by Builders Capture.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Long-Term Vision

Create the world's most trusted local intelligence infrastructure,
starting with Qeshm.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Design Principles

-   Mobile First
-   Offline First
-   AI Assisted
-   Human Verified
-   Community Powered
-   Privacy by Design

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Assumptions

-   Contributors are trusted community members.
-   Quality is more important than quantity.
-   Every observation should improve the knowledge base.
-   AI assists but never has final authority.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Non Goals

The product is NOT:

-   A social network
-   A marketplace
-   A messaging app
-   A tourism app
-   A CRM

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Success Definition

The system succeeds when verified knowledge continuously grows in
coverage, quality, freshness, and trust.
